## What Is a First Deposit Bonus?

A first deposit bonus is a special offer that online casino platforms provide to attract new players. When you deposit money into your account for the first time, the platform adds a percentage of your deposit as a bonus — typically ranging from 50% to 200%. This means depositing 500 BDT could earn you 500 to 1,000 BDT in bonus funds.

However, this bonus cannot be withdrawn directly. Every bonus comes with a **wagering requirement** (also called a rollover or playthrough) — a condition that must be met before any winnings from the bonus can be withdrawn. This guide explains everything in detail.

A wagering requirement (also called rollover or playthrough) is the amount you must bet before winnings from a bonus can be withdrawn. For example, a 10x wagering on a 1,000 BDT bonus means you must place bets totaling 10,000 BDT. Only after this is fulfilled can you withdraw.
